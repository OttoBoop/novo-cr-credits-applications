## Resumo

Programa da OpenAI que subsidia pesquisadores acadêmicos / nonprofits com até $1.000 em créditos da API.

## Critérios em verbatim

**Elegibilidade:**
> "Researcher(s) with an active affiliation to an academic institution or other research organization, as well as nonprofits conducting research activities (not operational support) are eligible to apply."

> "Applicants must be based in countries supported by our API."

**Foco priorizado:**
> "OpenAI is especially interested in subsidizing work from researchers with limited financial and institutional resources."

**Áreas de pesquisa de interesse** (do Granted AI mirror):
- Alignment
- Fairness & representation
- Societal impact
- Interdisciplinary research
- Interpretability / transparency
- Misuse potential
- Robustness

**Priority signal:**
> "Early-stage researcher with limited financial/institutional resources"

## O que oferece

- Até **$1.000** em créditos de API OpenAI
- Validade: **12 meses**
- Modelos: "publicly available models" (sem fine-tuning, sem experimentais)
- Créditos **não-renováveis** e **não-extensíveis**:
  > "API credits awarded through this program expire after 1 year, and cannot be extended or renewed."

## Ciclo

> "Applications are reviewed once every 3 months (in March, June, September, and December)"
> "the expected turnaround time for accepted applicants to receive their grant is around 4–6 weeks after the review for that quarter is complete."

**Próxima janela: junho 2026.**

## Critérios de avaliação

Priority signals (do Granted AI):
1. Active academic affiliation
2. Limited financial/institutional resources (early-stage researchers)
3. Research alignment with OpenAI's areas of interest

Avaliação:
- Mérito do projeto
- Caso de uso para a API
- Compliance com "sharing & publication policy" (preciso revisar antes de submeter)
